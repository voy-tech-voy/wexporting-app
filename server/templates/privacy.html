Privacy Policy for webatchify

Effective Date: 2026-02-13  |  Published by voy-tech apps

This Privacy Policy describes how voy-tech apps ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the webatchify Windows application (the "Application").

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other Personally Identifiable Information (PII).
  • No Telemetry: The Application does not send usage data, analytics, or crash reports to us or any third-party servers.
  • No File Uploads: All files you select for processing remain on your local device. We do not upload your images, videos, or other content to the cloud or any external server.

2. Local Processing

webatchify is designed as a local-only processing tool.

  • On-Device Processing: All image and video conversions, upscaling, and optimizations are performed entirely on your computer's CPU and/or GPU.
  • Data Isolation: Your files never leave your control. They are processed in memory and saved directly to the location you specify on your hard drive.

3. Network Activity

The Application connects exclusively to https://wavyvoy.pythonanywhere.com for the following purposes:

  • App update check — verifies whether a newer version is available (called once at startup).
  • Purchase receipt validation — confirms your Microsoft Store premium entitlement after a purchase event.
  • AI energy synchronisation — synchronises on-device AI processing credits with the server during authenticated sessions.

No user PII, file content, or personal data is included in any of these requests. All communication is encrypted via HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Libraries

The Application bundles open-source libraries including FFmpeg, ImageMagick, Real-ESRGAN, and RIFE. In this context, these libraries operate strictly as local processing engines and do not transmit data independently.

5. Permissions

The Application requires access to your File System solely to read the input files you select and write processed output files to your chosen destination. It does not scan your drive for other data.

6. User Rights

Although we do not collect or hold any personal data, we acknowledge your rights under GDPR and CCPA. Since all data resides locally on your device, you retain full control at all times. You can delete the Application at any time to remove all local traces.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated Effective Date.

9. Contact
